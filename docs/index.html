Features

Everything court reporters need

Purpose-built tools for converting, enhancing, and organizing deposition and courtroom recordings.

Smart Audio Cleanup

AI-powered noise removal, automatic leveling, de-clipping, de-reverb, and clarity enhancement make every word audible.

Court Format Support

Native support for SGMCA, TRM/FTR, BWF, and DM formats plus standard outputs: WAV, MP3, FLAC, Opus, and M4A.

Multi-Channel Control

Label channels by speaker role, adjust individual volumes, and preview tracks independently for precise mix control.

Five Processing Presets

Deposition, Phone Recording, Courtroom, Archive, and Quick Share presets tuned for real-world court reporting scenarios.

Multi-Source Merge

Synchronize and combine recordings captured from different devices into a single, cohesive output file.

Case Library

Organize recordings by case name, auto-detect CAT software jobs, and keep your entire workload structured and searchable.

AI Processing

Intelligent audio pipeline

Ten-stage processing chain transforms raw recordings into clear, professional audio ready for transcription.

Scan → Denoise → De-clip → HPF → Auto-Level → Normalize → De-reverb → Enhance → Trim → Fade

Requirements

System requirements

Lightweight and fast. FFmpeg is bundled so there is nothing extra to install.

macOS

  • macOS 12 Monterey or later
  • Native builds for Apple Silicon and Intel Macs
  • 4 GB RAM minimum, 8 GB recommended
  • FFmpeg bundled — no separate install

Windows

  • Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
  • 4 GB RAM minimum, 8 GB recommended
  • FFmpeg bundled — no separate install
